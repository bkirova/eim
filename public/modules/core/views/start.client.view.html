Instructions

Hello, and welcome to the Emotion in Motion experiment!

We would like you to wear some sensors that will monitor your heart and skin while you listen to three short pieces of music. After each piece, you will be asked some questions about how the music made you feel.

The songs that you will hear will be selected randomly.

The experiment will last approximately 15 minutes.

Please indicate your answers by selecting the relevant checkbox or clicking on the 5-point scale.

Checkbox:

Just click in the box to indicate your answer.

5-point Scale:

Click on one of the segments to select your answer.
